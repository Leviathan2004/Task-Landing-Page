Tony Chopper

This little reindeer gained the ability to change his form and think like humans after eating the Hito Hito no Mi fruit. Chopper is a valuable member of the Straw Hat Pirates crew, serving as the crew's doctor.

Roronoa Zoro

The first pirate (the second member if we count Luffy) to join the Straw Hat Pirates crew, Zoro accepted Luffy's invitation after the captain saved his life.

Monkey D. Luffy

"Straw Hat Luffy", as he became known, is the protagonist of the anime and the founder and captain of the Straw Hat Pirates crew. Since a very young age, his greatest dream has been to one day find the legendary treasure of Gol D. Roger, to become the new Pirate King.
